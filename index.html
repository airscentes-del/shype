What SHYPE is designed to become

1. Launch Profiles

Every token gets a clean page with mint, socials, chart, fee mode, staking badge, and verification links.

2. Fee Routing

Fees can be split into liquidity, SHYPE buyback/burn, staking vault, treasury, and creator share.

3. SHYPE Flywheel

Platform activity can support the platform token through transparent buybacks, burns, and vault reporting.

4. On-chain Verification

Every vault, burn, buyback, and launch should be linked to Solscan for trust and transparency.
