Privacy
Policy

Last revised on May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SHYPE handles information when you visit the website or use the experimental interface. The current public site is designed to be lightweight and does not require a user account.

1. Information you provide

If you enter token names, mint addresses, launch drafts, websites, social links, images, or other metadata into the interface, that information may be processed by your browser and may be visible to third-party services if you choose to submit, publish, or route it.

2. Wallet information

If wallet connection is enabled, your public wallet address may be visible to the site and to connected wallet or blockchain providers. SHYPE does not ask for seed phrases, private keys, recovery phrases, or wallet passwords.

3. Local storage

Some features may store preferences, tracked tokens, launch drafts, or interface state locally in your browser. Clearing browser data can remove this information. Local storage is not a secure place for private keys or confidential data.

4. Third-party services

The interface may rely on third-party wallets, RPC providers, APIs, swap venues, perps venues, analytics tools, hosting providers, or launch venues. Those services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Blockchain data

Transactions on public blockchains are public and may be permanent. If you sign or submit a transaction, wallet addresses, token transfers, metadata, and related activity may be visible to anyone.

6. Analytics and logs

The site may be hosted through services that collect basic technical logs such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, and request timestamps for security and reliability. If analytics are added later, this policy should be updated.

7. Security

SHYPE aims to keep the interface simple and non-custodial, but no website or protocol can be guaranteed secure. You should verify URLs, review wallet prompts, and avoid using wallets that hold funds you cannot afford to lose when testing experimental tools.

8. Contact

For privacy or repository-level questions, use the public repository: github.com/airscentes-del/shype.
